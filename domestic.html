Discover Tanzania, Your Way

Wendo Safaris invites all Tanzanians—students, families, teams, and communities—to explore our nation’s wildlife, cultures, and landscapes at affordable prices.

Affordable Adventures for Every Tanzanian

At Wendo Safaris, we’re passionate about bringing Tanzania’s breathtaking landscapes, vibrant cultures, and thrilling wildlife to every Tanzanian. Our tailored adventures are designed for students, schools, families, corporate teams, religious groups, and community organizations, fostering connection, learning, and pride in our shared heritage. From the vast plains of the Serengeti to the turquoise shores of Zanzibar, we invite you to explore Tanzania’s wonders in a way that suits your group’s spirit and interests.

What We Offer

  • Serengeti Wildlife Safari: Gather your school or team for an unforgettable journey through Serengeti National Park. Witness lions, elephants, and the great wildebeest migration during thrilling game drives. Enjoy campfire storytelling under starlit skies, perfect for bonding and creating lifelong memories.
  • Zanzibar Coastal Retreat: Ideal for families and religious groups, unwind on Zanzibar’s pristine white beaches. Snorkel vibrant coral reefs, wander through Stone Town’s historic Swahili alleys, and savor local spices, blending relaxation with cultural discovery.
  • Kilimanjaro Cultural Journey: Perfect for students and families, immerse yourself in the Chagga communities at Kilimanjaro’s foothills. Visit coffee farms, marvel at cascading waterfalls, and learn traditional crafts, fostering a deep connection to Tanzania’s cultural roots.
  • Ngorongoro Eco-Adventure: Youth groups and corporate teams will love exploring the Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Spot rhinos, zebras, and flamingos while learning about conservation efforts, combining adventure with environmental awareness.
  • Arusha Budget Camping: Designed for student and community groups, camp under the stars in Arusha National Park. Enjoy guided nature walks, birdwatching, and cozy campfire nights, offering an affordable way to connect with Tanzania’s natural beauty.
  • Lake Manyara Family Fun: Families and teams can marvel at tree-climbing lions and flamingo-filled lakes. Share group picnics and guided tours, creating joyful moments in one of Tanzania’s most scenic parks.
  • Tarangire Elephant Expedition: Perfect for all groups, discover Tarangire National Park’s majestic elephants and iconic baobab trees. Guided safaris and nature walks offer a blend of adventure and education, ideal for schools and corporate outings.
  • Bagamoyo Historical Escape: Religious groups and families can explore Bagamoyo’s rich history, from ancient Swahili ruins to colonial landmarks. Visit local markets and enjoy coastal walks, connecting with Tanzania’s storied past.
  • Selous Game Reserve Adventure: For community groups and teams, venture into the vast Selous Game Reserve. Experience boat safaris along the Rufiji River, spot hippos and crocodiles, and enjoy bush camping for a true wilderness escape.
  • Mikumi National Park Getaway: Affordable for students and families, Mikumi offers close-up wildlife encounters with giraffes, zebras, and antelopes. Short game drives and group activities make this perfect for quick, memorable trips.
  • Pemba Island Marine Adventure: Ideal for youth and religious groups, dive into Pemba’s turquoise waters for snorkeling and diving. Explore mangrove forests and local fishing villages, blending adventure with cultural immersion.
  • Mt. Meru Hiking Experience: Corporate teams and youth groups can tackle a guided trek on Mt. Meru in Arusha National Park. Enjoy stunning views, diverse flora, and team-building challenges, perfect for fostering unity and resilience.

Book your group adventure today and celebrate Tanzania’s beauty together! Contact us for customized packages tailored to your group’s needs and interests.
